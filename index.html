About Us

Welcome to
AS Glimps Studio

AS Glimps Studio is a professional Wedding Photography & Cinematography studio based in Kheriya Mod, Agra. We capture beautiful Indian weddings, pre-wedding shoots, engagement, haldi, mehendi, sangeet, baraat and reception celebrations with a cinematic storytelling approach.

Our aim is to preserve your precious emotions into timeless memories through creative photography, cinematic wedding films and premium editing. Every couple has a unique journey, and we capture every smile, ritual and celebration with passion.
